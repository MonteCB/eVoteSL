Welcome!

You have Logged into this system as a Secondary Administrator

Please read following Instructions carefully before proceeding to further steps.

  • You must use this system under the Rules and Regulations of Sri Lankan Government
  • In case of any purpose, DO NOT share your credentials(username/password) with others
  • Always logout from the system after you have done your work, or log in with an Incognito window; DO NOT save your passwords in any browser
  • Please note that the Election Commissioner has the power to remove your account instantly in any case of misuse of the system

You have privileges to do following actions in this system; you can access them with the side bar in the left side of the screen

  • Register Voters
  • Register Candidates
  • Register Poll Operators
  • Register Poll Booths
  • Add parties
  • Manage Voters and Candidates
  • Edit your profile and credentials

Instructions to proceed

  • Add all Parties before adding the Candidates
  • Candidate ID should be unique
  • Every poll operator should have a unique poll station and a username; this unique id is combined with only one booth.
    • Register Poll operators before registering the correcponding booth
    • When you Register the booth, select the poll station that you want to combine with that particular booth
    • Booth ID should be unique for each booth as well
  • The System is designed to achieve the above uniqueness of the features, In case of submitting an already added feature, the system will notify you to avoid such activities

----In case of a System failure, contact your Systems Engineer as soon as possible----
